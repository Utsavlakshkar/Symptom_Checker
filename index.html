Symptom Checker






This symptom checker aims to diagnose the disease suffered by the patient based on the symptoms entered by the patient.

The software will show you the list of symptoms and you have to enter either a single symptom or multiple symptoms.



This symptom checker uses Machine Learning models to predict the diseases based on the symtomps entered by the patient.
